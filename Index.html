We Are Demo Gym

Demo Gym was founded in 2019 with a single mission — to make world-class fitness accessible to everyone. We've grown from a small studio to the city's most well-equipped fitness centre, with over 3,200 active members and 12 elite trainers.

We don't believe in cookie-cutter plans. Every member gets a personalised journey — from custom workout programs to tailored nutrition advice. Our community is our biggest strength.

Open 24/5 — Early morning to late night batches
Air-conditioned throughout with premium equipment
Certified trainers with international credentials
In-house sports nutritionist on staff daily
